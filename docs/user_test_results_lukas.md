## B) Observed Issues & Delights

### Issues
*   **Integration Gap:** Lukas realized the SMART goal "Increase bookings" can't be automatically tracked because Houston isn't connected to his salon software. This breaks the promise of "tracking progress".
*   **Terminology:** "Playbooks" is a jargon term he didn't understand immediately. "Manual" or "Recipe" might be better for him.
*   **Expectation vs Reality:** He hoped "Marketing that gets done" meant *automation* (posting for him), not just *generating text*.

### Delights
*   **Speed:** The specific answer to his "empty slot" problem was instant and high quality.
*   **Price:** The clear "No credit card" and low entry price removed his barrier to entry.
