# User Testing Personas: Houston (AI Marketing Coach)

These personas represent the core target audience for Houston, ranging from marketing novices to professionals. They are used for simulated user testing to ensure the product works for different levels of marketing knowledge and technical affinity.

## 1. "Solo Coach Sandra"
* **Role:** Self-employed Life & Business Coach
* **Age:** 38
* **Background:** Former HR manager turned coach. Passionate about helping people but finds "selling herself" uncomfortable. Works alone from a home office.
* **Marketing Knowledge:** Beginner. Knows she needs "social media" and a newsletter but feels overwhelmed by the strategy.
* **Tech Proficiency:** Low-Medium. Can use Instagram and Zoom, but gets stuck with complex SaaS tools.
* **Goal with Houston:** Wants a "hand-holding" experience. Needs someone (or something) to tell her exactly what to post on Tuesday so she doesn't have to overthink it.
* **Fears:** "Is this just another tool I have to learn and then never use?" / "Will the AI sound like a robot and not like me?"

## 2. "Local Business Owner Lukas"
* **Role:** Owner of a high-end hair salon ("Lukas' Studio")
* **Age:** 45
* **Background:** Master hairdresser with 20 years of experience. His business runs on word-of-mouth, but he wants to attract a younger clientele.
* **Marketing Knowledge:** Very Low. Has a Facebook page he hasn't updated in 6 months. Thinks marketing is expensive ads.
* **Tech Proficiency:** Low. Uses his phone for everything. Desktop usage is rare.
* **Goal with Houston:** Wants more bookings/customers. Doesn't care about "strategy documents," just wants results.
* **Fears:** "I don't have time for this." / "Is this too complicated for me?"

## 3. "Marketing Manager Mia"
* **Role:** Marketing Manager at a mid-sized B2B software company (50 employees)
* **Age:** 32
* **Background:** Studied marketing. Is the only marketing person in the company, juggling events, content, and ads.
* **Marketing Knowledge:** High. Knows the jargon (SEO, funnel, retention).
* **Tech Proficiency:** High. Familiar with HubSpot, Canva, ChatGPT.
* **Goal with Houston:** Efficiency. Wants to automate the "boring stuff" (planning, basic copy) to focus on big campaigns. Needs a sparring partner.
* **Fears:** "Is the quality good enough for B2B?" / "Can I export this easily?" / "Will it hallucinatory nonsense?"

## 4. "Digital Creator Daniel"
* **Role:** Content Creator & Online Course Seller (Fitness for Dads)
* **Age:** 29
* **Background:** Built an audience on TikTok/Instagram. Now trying to monetize via email courses.
* **Marketing Knowledge:** Medium-High (specifically social & funnels).
* **Tech Proficiency:** Very High. Early adopter. Hates bad UI.
* **Goal with Houston:** Better conversion funnels. Wants specific, tactical advice to squeeze more revenue out of his list.
* **Fears:** "Is this just a wrapper around ChatGPT?" / "Is the UX smooth enough for my workflow?"

## 5. "Agency Owner Alex"
* **Role:** Owner of a boutique marketing agency (3 employees)
* **Age:** 40
* **Background:** Experienced marketer starting his own agency. Manages 5-10 clients simultaneously.
* **Marketing Knowledge:** Expert.
* **Tech Proficiency:** High.
* **Goal with Houston:** Scalability. Wants to use Houston to quickly generate strategies for new clients and standardized monthly plans.
* **Fears:** "Can I manage multiple workspaces?" / "Is the data secure?" / "Does the pricing scale?"
