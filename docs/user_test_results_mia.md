"I go straight to 'Strategy'.

### 4. Conclusion
"It's a strong productivity tool. It's not a 'Senior Strategist' replacement, but it's a great 'Junior Assistant'. The lack of integrations (HubSpot, GA4) is a blocker for 'real' data tracking, so I'd have to update goals manually. That's annoying. But for content ideation within context? Very good."

## B) Observed Issues & Delights

### Issues
*   **Data Silo:** As a pro, she immediately misses integrations with her existing stack (CRM, Analytics). Updating goals manually feels outdated.
*   **Credit Opacity:** While the landing page explained credits, she wonders how "complex" a chat has to be to count as "Deep Analysis". Is it automatic?

### Delights
*   **Context Awareness:** The fact that the chat remembered her "CTO" audience from onboarding without her repeating it was the biggest win.
*   **Playbooks:** She sees them as "SOPs" (Standard Operating Procedures) she can use to manage her team/interns.
